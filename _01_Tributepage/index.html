General Information

  • 28 October 1921

    Frederick Mayer was born in Freiburg im Breisgau, Baden, Germany, into a Jewish family.

  • 30 January 30 1933

    Adolf Hitler becomes German chancellor. Nazi Germany estalbishes first concentration camps.

  • 1938

    Mayer's family emigrates to the United States amid rising anti-Semitism.

  • 7 December 1941

    Japan attacks Pearl Harbor. Mayers englists in the US Army as trained in demolition, infiltration, raiding, sniping, and hand-to-hand combat. His training combined with his skills as a polyglot makes an a good candidate for Office of Strategic Services.

    Mayer teams up with 30 other operatives, conducting various operations in Austria. He becomes the commander of Operation Cleanup.

  • 26 February 1945

    Mayer and two other operatives parachute into Austria near Innsbruck, starting Operation Cleanup. He posed as a German Army officer and collected intelligene in Innsbruck. Laters posese as a French electrician.

    He is betrayed, captured, and tortured and eventually freed after American forces were orderd to take Innsbruck in 1945.

  • 14 April

    Mayers passes away at 94 in Charles town, West Virginia.
